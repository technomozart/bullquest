The 8 sectors

How it works

1
Back a sector
Stake GRIT on one of the 8 sectors with your bull. Your stake goes into the shared pool.
2
Win duels for it
Every duel you fight while backing counts. Your wins are what decide who controls the sector.
3
Split the pool
When the 3-day cycle ends, the winning sector's top backers split the pooled GRIT, minus a burn, and take the crown.

Rewards come only from staked GRIT, never printed. You must win at least one duel for your sector to earn a share.
