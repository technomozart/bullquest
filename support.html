Common questions

  • How do I delete my account? Open your account menu and choose "Delete account". This permanently removes your account and data.
  • How do I get a free bull? Every new account gets one free bull and 200 GRIT the moment you sign up. No purchase needed.
  • What is GRIT? GRIT is the in-game score you earn by playing. It has no cash value.
  • Is BullQuest free? Yes. The whole game loop (duels, the daily spin, quests, the gym) is free to play.
